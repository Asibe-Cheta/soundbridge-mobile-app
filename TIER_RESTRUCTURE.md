## Overview

SoundBridge operates on a freemium model with two tiers designed to convert users at critical professional workflow moments. The structure balances accessibility (robust free tier) with conversion incentives (removing pain points at upgrade).

**No Free Trial Strategy:** SoundBridge does NOT offer a 7-day free trial. Instead, the generous Free tier acts as an unlimited trial period, and Pro upgrades come with a **7-day money-back guarantee**. This prevents exploitation while still offering risk-free evaluation.

### Pricing Summary

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Savings |
|------|---------|--------|---------|
| **Free** | ¬£0 | ¬£0 | - |
| **Pro** | ¬£9.99/mo | ¬£99/yr | ¬£20.88 (17% discount) |

**Annual Conversion Message:** "Save ¬£20 - that's 2 months free!"

**Money-Back Guarantee:** All Pro upgrades include a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within 7 days of payment, request a full refund - no questions asked.

## Money-Back Guarantee & Refund Policy

### Why No Free Trial?

**SoundBridge does NOT offer a traditional 7-day free trial.** Instead, we use a superior model:

1. **Free Tier = Unlimited Trial**
   - No credit card required
   - No time limits
   - Full access to test core features (3 uploads, searches, messaging)
   - Users can evaluate platform value before committing

2. **Pro Upgrade = 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee**
   - Pay upfront (¬£9.99 monthly or ¬£99 annually)
   - If not satisfied within 7 days ‚Üí full refund, no questions asked
   - Account reverts to Free tier
   - Your work is protected (see below)

### Why This Approach?

**Prevents Exploitation:**
- ‚úÖ Credit card requirement creates commitment friction
- ‚úÖ Payment collection discourages "serial trial users"
- ‚úÖ Refund process requires deliberate action (stops casual exploitation)
- ‚úÖ Tracks refund patterns to identify abuse

**Better Economics:**
- ‚úÖ Collect revenue upfront (vs. ¬£0 during trial period)
- ‚úÖ Refund rate typically 3-5% (95%+ users keep subscription)
- ‚úÖ Higher conversion than traditional trials (payment = psychological commitment)

**Simpler User Experience:**
- ‚úÖ No confusing "trial limits vs. full Pro limits"
- ‚úÖ Clear path: Free ‚Üí Upgrade ‚Üí Money-back guarantee
- ‚úÖ Aligns with industry best practices (Netflix, Spotify, Adobe all moved away from free trials)

### Refund Process

**User requests refund within 7 days of payment:**

**Step 1: Cancellation Confirmation**
```
"Are you sure you want to cancel?

You're within your 7-day money-back guarantee period.

[Keep Pro Subscription] [Process Refund & Cancel]"
```

**Step 2: Track Selection (if user has >3 uploads)**
```
"We're sorry to see you go!

Your refund of ¬£9.99 will be processed within 3-5 business days.
Your account will revert to Free tier, which includes 3 track uploads.

You currently have 8 tracks uploaded. Which 3 would you like to keep public?

[Select 3 tracks to keep]

Note: Your other tracks will be private (not deleted). You can restore them
anytime by upgrading to Pro again."
```

**Step 3: Confirmation & Processing**
- Stripe refund processed (3-5 business days)
- User tier ‚Üí 'free'
- Selected 3 tracks remain public
- Remaining tracks ‚Üí `visibility: 'private'`
- Confirmation email sent
- Optional exit survey (to improve product)

### What Happens to Uploaded Tracks?

**Critical: Tracks are NEVER deleted during refund/downgrade process**

**Scenario 1: User Upgrades to Pro, Uploads 10 Tracks, Requests Refund**
- Full refund processed ‚úÖ
- User selects 3 tracks to keep public
- Remaining 7 tracks ‚Üí private (hidden from public, accessible in user's dashboard)
- If user re-upgrades later ‚Üí all 10 tracks automatically public again

**Scenario 2: Free User Has 3 Tracks, Upgrades to Pro, Adds 7 More, Then Downgrades**
- User selects which 3 tracks stay public (can be any of the 10)
- Other 7 ‚Üí private
- No data loss, just visibility change

**Track Visibility States:**
- `public` - Visible to everyone, appears in search/profile
- `private` - Only visible to user, hidden from public
- `unlisted` - Accessible via direct link only (future feature)

### Preventing Serial Refund Abuse

**Refund Tracking System:**
- Track refund history by:
  - Email address
  - Payment method (last 4 digits)
  - IP address (for pattern detection)
  - Device fingerprint

**Abuse Prevention Rules:**

**1st Refund:**
- ‚úÖ Processed automatically, no questions asked
- ‚úÖ User maintains good standing

**2nd Refund (same user):**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Manual review triggered
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Email: "We notice this is your second refund. Is there something we can help with?"
- ‚úÖ Refund still processed, but flagged

**3rd+ Refund (same user):**
- ‚ùå No more money-back guarantee
- Email: "We notice you've requested multiple refunds. Unfortunately, we can no longer offer the money-back guarantee on future subscriptions. However, you can continue using our Free tier."
- User can still upgrade to Pro, but no refund option

**Multiple Account Detection:**
- If same payment method used across multiple accounts ‚Üí flag as suspicious
- If same IP address with multiple refunds ‚Üí flag for review
- Stripe's built-in fraud detection catches most patterns

### Edge Cases

**Edge Case 1: Annual Subscriber Requests Refund After 5 Days**
- Same 7-day money-back guarantee applies
- Full ¬£99 refund processed
- Account reverts to Free tier
- Track selection process same as monthly

**Edge Case 2: User Requests Refund After 8 Days**
- Outside 7-day window
- No refund, but can cancel subscription
- Pro benefits continue until end of billing period
- Then account downgrades to Free tier

**Edge Case 3: Payment Fails After Initial Success**
- Grace period: 7 days to update payment method
- Email notifications: Days 1, 3, 5, 7
- After 7 days ‚Üí subscription cancelled, account ‚Üí Free tier
- Track selection process initiated automatically

**Edge Case 4: User Disputes Charge with Bank (Chargeback)**
- Stripe handles dispute process
- Account immediately suspended pending resolution
- If chargeback successful ‚Üí account ‚Üí Free tier (no track selection, system auto-selects first 3)
- If chargeback fails ‚Üí account restored
- User flagged for future fraud prevention

## Copyright Handling (All Tiers)

Instead of AI detection, SoundBridge uses:

- **User Certification:** At upload, users certify they own rights or have permission
- **DMCA Takedown Process:** Standard process for copyright claims
- **Community Reporting:** Users can report copyright violations
- **Terms of Service Enforcement:** Violations result in content removal and potential account suspension
- **Three-Strike Policy:** Repeated violations lead to permanent ban
- **Appeal Process:** Users can appeal copyright strikes with proof of ownership

**Philosophy:** Users manage their own copyright (like SoundCloud), platform responds to valid claims

### FAQ Section

**Do you offer a free trial?**

We don't offer a traditional free trial because our Free tier IS your trial! You get unlimited time to test SoundBridge with 3 uploads, professional searches, and messaging - no credit card required.

When you're ready to upgrade to Pro, you're protected by our 7-day money-back guarantee. If Pro isn't right for you, request a refund within 7 days for a full refund, no questions asked.

**Can I change my plan later?**

Yes! You can upgrade from Free to Pro anytime. If you start with monthly, you can switch to annual to save money. You can also cancel anytime - if you're within the first 7 days, you'll get a full refund.

**What happens when I hit my limits on Free?**

You'll see a notification letting you know you've reached your limit (searches or messages). You can wait until next month when limits reset, or upgrade to Pro anytime for unlimited access.

**Can I cancel anytime?**

Absolutely. You can cancel your Pro subscription anytime:
- **Within 7 days of payment:** Full refund, account reverts to Free tier
- **After 7 days:** No refund, but Pro benefits continue until end of billing period, then account reverts to Free tier

**What payment methods do you accept?**

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) via Stripe. Your payment information is secure, encrypted, and never stored on our servers.

**How does the 7-day money-back guarantee work?**

If you upgrade to Pro and decide it's not for you within 7 days of payment, simply request a refund from your billing settings. You'll receive a full refund within 3-5 business days, no questions asked. Your account will revert to the Free tier.

**What happens to my content if I cancel or request a refund?**

Your content is never deleted. If you have more than 3 tracks when you downgrade to Free, you'll choose which 3 to keep public. The rest become private (only visible to you). If you upgrade to Pro again later, all your tracks automatically become public again.

**What if I uploaded 10 tracks on Pro and then cancel?**

When you cancel or request a refund, you'll select which 3 tracks to keep public (Free tier limit). Your other 7 tracks will be private - still accessible to you in your dashboard, but hidden from public view. If you re-upgrade to Pro, they'll automatically become public again. Your music is never deleted.

**Can I get a refund after 7 days?**

The money-back guarantee only applies within 7 days of payment. After 7 days, you can cancel your subscription, but won't receive a refund. Your Pro benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.

**How do I request a refund?**

Go to Account Settings ‚Üí Billing ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. If you're within your 7-day window, you'll see the option to request a full refund. Click it, follow the prompts, and your refund will be processed automatically.

## Financial Impact Analysis: Money-Back Guarantee vs. Free Trial

### Revenue Comparison Model

#### Traditional 7-Day Free Trial Model:
- 500 users start free trial (no payment)
- Trial conversion rate: 40-50% (industry standard)
- Actual paying users after trial: 200-250
- Revenue (assuming 225 convert): 225 √ó ¬£9.99 = ¬£2,247.75/month

**Problems with Free Trial:**
- ‚ùå No revenue during trial period
- ‚ùå Users procrastinate ("I'll try it later")
- ‚ùå Can exploit system (upload 10 tracks, cancel before payment)
- ‚ùå 50-60% of trials never convert
- ‚ùå Support costs for users who never pay
- ‚ùå 7-day delay before revenue recognition

#### SoundBridge Money-Back Guarantee Model:
- 500 users upgrade directly (pay upfront)
- Refund rate: 3-5% (industry standard for money-back guarantees)
- Actual paying users: 475-485 (assuming 5% refund rate = 475)
- Revenue: 475 √ó ¬£9.99 = ¬£4,745.25/month
- **Annual Revenue: ¬£56,943**

**Benefits of Money-Back Guarantee:**
- ‚úÖ **111% more revenue** than free trial model
- ‚úÖ Immediate cash flow (payment on day 1, not day 8)
- ‚úÖ Payment commitment = higher quality users
- ‚úÖ Lower support burden (paying users more serious)
- ‚úÖ Prevents exploitation (refund process creates friction)
- ‚úÖ Better user selection (casual browsers filtered out)

---

### Why Money-Back Guarantees Convert Better

**Psychological Factors:**

**1. Loss Aversion**
- Once money leaves account, users reluctant to "lose" access
- Free trial mindset: "I haven't committed anything yet"
- Paid with guarantee mindset: "I've invested, let me get value from this"

**2. Sunk Cost Fallacy (Works in Your Favor)**
- User paid ¬£9.99 ‚Üí psychologically wants to use it
- Requesting refund requires active effort ‚Üí most don't bother if getting any value
- Free trial cancellation is passive (just don't enter payment info)

**3. Selection Bias**
- Free trials attract casual browsers and tire-kickers
- Payment requirement filters for serious, committed users
- Higher intent at signup = higher retention long-term

**4. Immediacy**
- Free trial: "I'll explore this later" ‚Üí never return
- Paid: "I paid for this, let me use it NOW" ‚Üí immediate engagement

**Real-World Evidence:**
- **Netflix** removed free trial (2020) ‚Üí subscriber growth increased
- **Spotify** requires payment method for trials ‚Üí 60%+ conversion
- **Adobe** moved to paid trials ‚Üí retention up 30%
- **Amazon Prime** trial requires payment ‚Üí 75%+ conversion
