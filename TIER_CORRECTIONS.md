### FAQ: Upload Limits

**Q: Do my uploads expire?**
A: No! All uploads stay on your profile forever (within storage limit).

**Q: If I upload 5 tracks in January, do they disappear in February?**
A: No! They stay. You just get 10 NEW upload slots in February.

**Q: What if I don't use all 10 uploads this month?**
A: Unused slots don't carry over. You get fresh 10 slots next month.

**Q: Can I upload 20 tracks if I skip a month?**
A: No. You get 10 slots per month maximum, regardless of previous months.

**Q: What happens if I downgrade from Pro to Free?**
A: You choose 3 tracks to keep public. Others become private until you upgrade again.

**Q: If I delete a track, does it free up storage?**
A: Yes! Deleting tracks frees storage for new uploads.

**Q: Can I re-upload a deleted track?**
A: Yes, if you have upload slots available and storage space.

**Q: Do I need Enterprise for unlimited uploads?**
A: No Enterprise tier for Year 1. 10/month should be plenty for 95%+ of users. If you consistently need more, contact support.
